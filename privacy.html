1 Overview

Faceless Factory ("we," "our," or "us") operates an automated AI content publishing application ("Application"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Application.

By using Faceless Factory, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Application.

Key Commitment

We do not sell your personal data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

2 Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account credentials: Email address when you register
  • Configuration data: Content niche, posting schedule, platform preferences
  • API keys: Encrypted credentials for AI services you choose to connect

  • Usage logs: Actions taken within the Application (video created, published, failed)
  • Performance data: Video analytics (views, likes, CTR) fetched from platform APIs
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, operating system (for security purposes)
  • Error logs: Crash reports and error messages for debugging

3 How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Authenticate your accounts and publish content to connected platforms
  • Content generation: Generate AI scripts and thumbnails based on your niche preferences
  • Scheduling: Post content at optimal times configured by you
  • Analytics: Retrieve and display performance metrics from your connected platforms
  • Optimization: Improve AI-generated content quality based on performance data
  • Security: Detect and prevent unauthorized access or abuse
  • Communication: Send operational notifications (publish success/failure, token expiry)
  • Legal compliance: Comply with applicable laws and platform terms of service

We do not use your data for targeted advertising or sell it to data brokers.

4 TikTok API Data Usage

TikTok Specific Disclosure

This section specifically addresses how we handle data obtained through the TikTok API in compliance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service.

4.1 Data We Access via TikTok API

  • Access tokens: OAuth tokens used to authenticate API requests on your behalf
  • Basic profile info: TikTok username and account ID (to identify the publishing account)
  • Video upload permission: The video.upload and video.publish scopes

4.2 What We Do NOT Access

  • We do not access your TikTok followers list, messages, or personal content
  • We do not read, download, or store your existing TikTok videos
  • We do not access your TikTok payment information
  • We do not access your contact list or device information via TikTok

4.3 TikTok Data Retention

TikTok access tokens are stored encrypted in our database and are used solely to publish videos on your behalf. Tokens are deleted immediately when you disconnect your TikTok account or delete your Faceless Factory account. We do not retain TikTok data beyond what is necessary to provide the publishing service.

4.4 No Sale or Transfer of TikTok Data

Data obtained through TikTok APIs is not sold, transferred, or shared with any third party for any purpose other than facilitating publishing on your TikTok account as authorized by you.

6 Data Storage & Security

6.1 Where Data is Stored

Your data is stored locally on the device running the Faceless Factory application (SQLite database). When using cloud deployment, data resides on your chosen hosting provider's servers.

6.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption at rest: API keys and OAuth tokens are encrypted before storage
  • Encrypted transmission: All API communications use HTTPS/TLS
  • Access control: Application protected by user-configured authentication
  • No cloud storage of video content: Videos are processed locally and uploaded directly to platforms

6.3 Security Limitations

No system is 100% secure. We implement industry-standard security measures but cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that may affect your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

7 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With platforms you authorize: Content is published to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook only as directed by you
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Safety: To prevent fraud, illegal activity, or harm to users
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition, with prior notice to you

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

8 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Your Rights
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Disconnect platform accounts at any time

Revoking Platform Access

You can revoke Faceless Factory's access to your social media accounts at any time:

  • TikTok: TikTok App → Privacy → Apps and Websites → Faceless Factory → Remove
  • YouTube/Google: myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party apps → Revoke
  • Instagram/Facebook: Settings → Apps and Websites → Faceless Factory → Remove

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@faceless-factory.app.

10 Children's Privacy

Faceless Factory is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information promptly.
