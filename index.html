How It Works

From idea to published
in minutes

1

Connect Your Accounts

Authorize Faceless Factory to access your TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook accounts via official OAuth flows.

2

Configure Your Niche

Choose your content niche, language, posting schedule, and platform preferences. The AI adapts to your brand voice.

3

AI Creates Content

Gemini AI generates trending scripts. The system adds voiceover, stock footage, subtitles, and an AI thumbnail automatically.

4

Review & Approve

Preview generated content before publishing. Manual approval mode lets you review every video, or enable auto-publish for full automation.

5

Publish Everywhere

Videos are automatically published to all configured platforms at optimal posting times for maximum reach.
